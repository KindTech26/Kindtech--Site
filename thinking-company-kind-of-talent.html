the Kind of talent

21 Fev

At Kind Tech, we were born, iterate, and evolve with a clear ambition not to be just another tech startup. We position ourselves as a talent hub with national relevance and European ambition. We believe that the true competitive advantage in Artificial Intelligence is not only technical. It is cultural.

Portugal offers a unique context. Beyond the growing exposure to entrepreneurship, there is a structural characteristic that has shaped us for decades. We learned to compete with fewer resources. That built a culture of efficiency, critical thinking, and a strong focus on optimization. We know how to prioritize. We know how to simplify. We know how to execute under constraint.

In the AI market, this mindset is essential. The very paradox of Artificial Intelligence demands greater control, higher efficiency, and disciplined technical decisions. At Kind Tech, we develop applied solutions that require doing more with less. More impact with less waste. More control with less complexity.

We look for a specific layer of talent. People who have tried to build. Who have taken risks. Who have failed and learned early. We do not value perfect careers. We value entrepreneurial maturity, execution capability, and comfort with uncertainty.

We do not compete only for technical skill. We compete for mindset. That combination of structural Portuguese efficiency and entrepreneurial spirit defines who we are and what we are building.

Humberto Bastos,
CEO Kind Tech
